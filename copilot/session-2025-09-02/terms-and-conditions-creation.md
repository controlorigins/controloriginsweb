### **Key Legal Requirements for Static Sites with Analytics**

Based on research from Microsoft documentation and industry standards:

1. **Privacy and Data Collection Transparency**

   - Cookie usage notification

   - Professional advice disclaimers

3. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - GDPR compliance considerations
   - Data protection statements
   - Regulatory consulting disclaimers (SOX, HIPAA, PCI)

4. **Standard Legal Protections**
   - Intellectual property protection
   - External links disclaimers
   - Modification rights
   - Governing law clauses
